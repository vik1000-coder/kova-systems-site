Privacy notice

Privacy at Kova Systems

This notice describes the public Kova Systems website. Product pilots or future document-processing services will require additional, product-specific notices and agreements.

Effective: July 21, 2026 · Applies to this public website and general inquiries.

1. Who we are

Kova Systems (“Kova Systems,” “we,” “us,” or “our”) develops evidence-centered software, including Aver and PrepIEP. This notice applies to the Kova Systems public website and messages sent to our general contact addresses.

2. Information we collect

Information you provide

We may receive your name, email address, organization, inquiry topic, message, and any other information you choose to include when you contact us.

Basic technical information

This website does not install analytics or advertising cookies. Our hosting, domain, email, security, or content-delivery providers may nevertheless process standard technical records such as IP address, browser type, requested page, timestamps, and security logs as part of operating their services.

3. Information you should not send

Do not send student education records, IEPs, evaluations, disability or health information, information identifying a child, government identifiers, financial account information, passwords, API keys, confidential production data, or other sensitive information through the public website or general email.

4. Children and student information

The Kova Systems website is intended for adults. It is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children under 13 through this website. PrepIEP is available at prepiep.com and has its own product terms and privacy practices.

If you believe a child has provided personal information through this website, contact us so we can investigate and, where appropriate, delete it.

5. How we use information

  • Respond to inquiries and communicate about requested pilots, research, or partnerships.
  • Understand potential customer and user needs.
  • Operate, secure, troubleshoot, and improve the website and our communications.
  • Prevent misuse, fraud, or security incidents.
  • Comply with applicable law and protect legal rights.

6. How we disclose information

We may disclose information to service providers that support hosting, domain management, email, security, and business operations; to professional advisers; in connection with a financing, acquisition, reorganization, or sale; or when required to comply with law, protect rights, or investigate misuse. We do not sell personal information collected through this website and do not use it for targeted advertising.

7. Retention

We retain inquiry information only as long as reasonably necessary to respond, maintain business records, evaluate a potential relationship, resolve disputes, secure our systems, and meet legal obligations. Product pilots should define their own retention and deletion terms in a separate agreement.

8. Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures appropriate to the information we hold. No transmission or storage method is completely secure. The most important protection for this public site is data minimization: please do not send sensitive information.

9. Your choices

You may ask us to access, correct, or delete inquiry information, or to stop non-essential communications, subject to legal and operational exceptions. We may need to verify your request.

10. Third-party services and links

External sites and service providers have their own privacy practices. This notice does not govern them. This website is hosted on GitHub Pages, which may process standard technical logs as part of operating the service.

11. Changes

We may update this notice as the website, products, or law changes. The effective date above will identify the current version. Material changes should be communicated through the website or another appropriate channel.

12. Contact

Privacy questions may be sent to contact@kovasystemsllc.com. Do not include sensitive records in your request.
