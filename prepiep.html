Kova Systems / PrePIEP
Private development · no document uploads

Prepare for an IEP meeting with the whole record in view.

PrePIEP is an adult-facing document-understanding and meeting-preparation tool designed to help parents trace whether documented needs are connected to present levels, goals, services, and measured progress.

Join parent researchRead the boundaries

The preparation problem

Do not just summarize the IEP. Trace the logic.

A long record can contain each required piece while still leaving important gaps between what was observed, what was planned, and what was measured.

Documented needsWhat evaluations, observations, and records say the student needs.
Present levelsHow current functioning and baseline performance are described.
GoalsWhat measurable change the plan says it will pursue.
ServicesWhat supports, frequency, setting, and responsible parties are specified.
ProgressWhat evidence will show whether the plan is working.

Intended workflow

From a stack of documents to a meeting plan.

The product direction is a structured, longitudinal, evidence-linked preparation system—not a one-time chatbot summary.

Organize the record

Bring evaluations, prior plans, progress reports, and parent notes into a consistent chronology.

Identify missing connections

Flag where a documented need has no clear goal, service, baseline, or progress measure linked to it.

Prepare source-linked questions

Generate prioritized questions and follow-up actions that point back to the relevant source material.

Product boundaries

Preparation support, not professional judgment.

These limits are part of the product definition and should remain visible as PrePIEP develops.

PrePIEP may support

  • Document organization and plain-language explanation
  • Cross-document consistency checks
  • Evidence-linked meeting questions
  • Tracking goals, services, and reported progress over time
  • Preparing a parent to ask better-informed questions

PrePIEP does not provide

  • Legal advice or legal conclusions
  • Medical, clinical, or educational diagnosis
  • Eligibility, placement, or service determinations
  • A substitute for an attorney, advocate, clinician, or educator
  • Direct services to children or users under 18
No sensitive document uploads are enabled on this company site.

Until product-specific security, privacy, terms, data handling, and insurance controls are in place, visitors should not send IEPs, evaluations, student names, disability information, or other education records through the contact form or email.
