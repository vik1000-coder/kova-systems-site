Operating principles

Evidence before confidence.

We do not treat a polished output as proof that a system is correct.

Defined scope

Every result should say what was evaluated, what was not evaluated, and what decision it can reasonably support.

Source linkage

Important claims should remain connected to the documents, examples, or tests that produced them.

Human judgment

Our tools support preparation and evaluation. They do not replace the people responsible for educational, legal, clinical, or production decisions.

Data restraint

Collect less, retain less, and avoid sensitive uploads until the product and its safeguards are ready for them.
