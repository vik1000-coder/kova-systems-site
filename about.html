About Kova Systems

Software that makes the basis for a decision easier to inspect.

Kova Systems is a Massachusetts-based software company in formation, developing evidence-centered tools for AI reliability and complex document preparation.

Why Kova exists

Consequential software should show its work.

Many tools optimize for a fast answer. Kova Systems focuses on the surrounding decision system: the source material, evaluation standard, differences that matter, unresolved uncertainty, and the human review that follows.

That principle connects two otherwise different products. Aver supports model-change decisions by preserving comparison evidence. PrePIEP supports meeting preparation by preserving document evidence and the links between needs, plans, and progress.

Company principles

Build trust through bounded claims.

Start with a concrete decision

Build around a specific choice a person or team must make, not around an abstract promise that AI will solve the domain.

Expose the evidence path

Keep outputs connected to source documents, test cases, model settings, and review criteria.

State what is not covered

Trust improves when users can see the boundaries of the evaluation and the conditions under which a result may fail.

Earn the right to handle sensitive data

Do not collect high-risk records before the product has appropriate operational, contractual, and technical safeguards.

Current portfolio

Deliberately narrow stages.

Kova Systems is not claiming two fully launched businesses. The products are at different stages and are presented accordingly.

Active pilot development

Aver

The active product experiment: model-change assurance for people and teams that need to improve cost or quality without silently degrading behavior.

Private development
